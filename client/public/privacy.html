Chkobba Café is a casual multiplayer card game. This page explains what information is stored by the site and what third parties may collect when ads are enabled.

What we store

  • Profile info you choose: nickname and avatar selection.
  • Your cookie consent choice (accept/decline) to remember your preference.

Ads (Google AdSense)

If you accept cookies in the in-game banner, we may load Google AdSense. AdSense may set cookies and collect information to personalize or measure ads.

If you decline cookies, ads won’t be loaded.

Learn more: How Google uses data.

Bug reports / contact

If you submit a bug report via the linked form in the game, the information you provide is sent to that form provider.

Your choices

  • You can decline cookies and continue playing.
  • You can change your choice anytime via the “Cookies” link in the footer.

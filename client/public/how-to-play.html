Last updated: 2026-01-14

Turn basics

  1. Select a card from your hand.
  2. Optionally select table cards that make an exact sum.
  3. Press Play Selected.
  4. If you can’t capture, your card is placed on the table.

Capturing rules (high level)

  • You can capture with an exact single-card match.
  • You can also capture by selecting a set of cards whose sum equals your card.
  • If an exact match exists, it takes priority over sums.

Scoring overview

Points are awarded at the end of each round. Common bonuses include most cards, most diamonds, capturing the 7♦, and chkobba (clearing the table).

Playing with friends

  • Create a room and share the room code.
  • Open the site in 4 tabs/devices and join the same code.
  • Edit your nickname/avatar so your seat is recognizable.

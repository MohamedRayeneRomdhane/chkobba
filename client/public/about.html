What is Chkobba?

Chkobba is a classic Tunisian card game played with an Italian deck. The goal is to capture cards from the table and score points across rounds — with a bonus for making a chkobba (clearing the table).

What is this website?

Chkobba Café is a lightweight online version designed for friendly play with friends. Rooms use short codes (private by default): create a room, share the code, and join from multiple devices.

  • Fast multiplayer rooms (4 players, 2v2).
  • Works on desktop and mobile.
  • Includes an in-game tutorial and end-of-round scoring breakdown.
